Here at Post Wardrobe, we aim to help you get the most out of your favorite items of clothing. Have a bit-too-pricey prom dress you can’t completely let go of just yet? How about a bridesmaid’s dress that would break your best friends heart if you donated or sold forever? Make a “Vendor” account here on PW, upload some photos and a description of the item, and add your 2-week-rental price. It’s easy as pie!
If you are looking to rent out or buy one of these beautiful items for yourself, make a “Shopper” account, check off your style preferences and location settings, and voila! You have access to hundreds of styles while being eco-friendly and bargain savvy. Whether you’re selling, buying, or renting, the Post Wardrobe community is here so you make the most of the latest trends, the older classics, and the unique style of others.
